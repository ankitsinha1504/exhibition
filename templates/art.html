Mona Lisa

- Leonardo Da Vinci , 1519

  • • The name of that lady is Lisa del Giocondo, the wife of Francesco del Giocondo.
  • • This painting is painted as an oil painting on wood.
  • • It became famous when it was stolen in summer 1911.

The Starry Night

- Vincent Van Gogh , 1889

  • • It represents the view from his room just before the sunrise with the addition of the village of Saint-Remy-de-Provence.
  • • Since 1941, it has been in the collection of the Museum of Modern Art in New York City.

The Harvesters

- Pieter Bruegel the Elder , 1565

  • • It was extraordinary for a time when landscapes served mostly as backdrops for religious paintings.
  • • A group of peasants on the left cut and bundle ripened wheat, while the on the right, another group takes their midday meal.

Irises

- Vincent Van Gogh , 1889

  • • He started this painting within one week when he was admitted to an asylum.
  • • He named this painting “The lightning conductor for my illness” because he felt that continuing painting keep himself secured from going insane.

Lady With An Ermine

- Leonardo Da Vinci , 1491

  • • The name of that lady in the painting is Cecilia Gallerani who was the mistress of Ludovico Sforza, Duke of Milan at that time.
  • • It is an Oil Painting on Walnut board.
  • • One of Poland’s national treasures.

Napoleon Crossing the Alps

- Jacques-Louis David , 1805

  • • It was inspired by Napoleon's victory at the Battle of Marengo.
  • • Napolean refused to sit for the portrait.
  • • Napolean liked the painting so much that he wanted David to make this exact composition three more times.

The Storm On The Sea Of Galilee

- Rembrandt , 1633

  • • The paintings show the miracle of Jesus calming the storm on the Sea of Galilee.
  • • On March 18, 1990, thieved masked themselves as police officers and entered the museum and stole the paintings.
  • • The museum still displays the empty frames in their original location.

Guernica

- Pablo Picasso , 1937

  • This painting represents the suffering of people wrenched by violence and chaos.
  • The painting became popular and it helped to bring worldwide attention to Spanish warfare.
  • Outstanding within the composition is a gored horse, a bull, and flames.

Cafe Terrace at Night

- Vincent Van Gogh, 1888

  • • It is also known as The Cafe Terrace on the Place du Forum.
  • • The visitors to the cafe can stand at the north-eastern corner of the Place du Forum.
  • • The cafe was refurbished in 1990-1991 to replicate Van Gogh’s painting.

Saturn Devouring His Son

- Goya , 1823

  • • It represents the Greek myth of the Titan Cronus who had fear of thrown by one of his children.
  • • He ate them one by one upon their birth.
